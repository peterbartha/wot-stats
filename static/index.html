WN8 reference

This website calculate World of Tanks players WN8 rating, and assigns a different color according to the official WN8 specification at wnefficiency.net.

You can see the table of expected tanks value used to calculate your WN8 rating at this page. The current table version WoTstats.org is now using is the v27.

About WotStats

Welcome to WoTStats, a World of Tanks Statistics Tracker. Here you can track any World of Tanks players statistics and WN8 progression in a nice looking way. Statistics includes also medals and detailed tanks stats with changes over time. You can update your stats whenever you want.
